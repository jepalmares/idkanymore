Hope



Meaning of Hope




Hope is an optimistic state of mind that is based on an expectation
of positive outcomes with respect to events and circumstances
in one's life or the world at large. As a verb, its definitions include:
"expect with confidence" and "to cherish a desire with anticipation."


Cause of Having Hope

Connections with proper, positive people, a higher power,
and the web of life. Regeneration in all forms as stimulated
by breathing, sleep, meditation, prayer, spirit, impermanence,
and quiet. Witnessing or experiencing acts of love,
goodness, and kindness.

Effects of Having Hope

Hope is associated with many positive outcomes, including
greater happiness, better academic achievement and even
lowered risk of death. It's a necessary ingredient
for getting through tough times, of course,
but also for meeting everyday goals.
